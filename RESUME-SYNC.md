2. Compare the revised resume with the website's employment, project, education, skill, certification, and contact facts.
3. Update `index.html`, `timeline.html`, `education.html`, `projects.html`, the three `case-study-*.html` pages, and `resume.html` where the new facts apply.
4. Preserve content that is not resume-derived, including coaching and technical interests, unless the new source explicitly changes it.
5. Keep confidential automation source locations and project-sensitive material off the public site.
6. Recheck local links, scripts, responsive layout, and every remaining `[REVIEW]` marker.
7. Record the new import date and checksum in this file.

Project photos, diagrams, coaching dates, and coaching outcomes are maintained separately because the resume does not verify those assets or details.
