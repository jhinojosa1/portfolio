Soil solidification / Field operations

Venture Global CP2 LNG Soil Solidification

Project overview

Controlling quality at production scale.

As a Field Engineer in Cameron Parish, Louisiana, I managed Portland cement usage and stabilized-soil production for a six-month, $86 million scope.

The operation treated approximately 2.8 million cubic yards of soil and required disciplined material control, verification, reporting, and schedule tracking.

RoleField Engineer
Scale$86M · 2.8M cubic yards
DatesJun–Dec 2025

The challenge

Maintain treatment quality and material accountability while soil production moved at large scale across a demanding six-month schedule.

02

My responsibility

I managed cement usage and production tracking and verified application rates, treatment depth, moisture conditioning, mixing, compaction, and density testing.

03

Field control

When testing or field conditions did not meet project requirements, I directed corrective work while maintaining the reporting and schedule information needed to keep the operation aligned.
